Blaze broke out in Tsz Wan Shan, three sent to hospital

Blaze broke out in Tsz Wan Shan, three sent to hospital

發佈日期: 2025-12-01 13:07
TVB News
已複製連結
A fire broke out at Tsz Wan Shan in the early hours of Monday.

Three people were sent to the hospital for treatment due to smoke inhalation. The building's fire alarms failed to go off.

Burn marks are left on the wall of the affected unit at Oi Wing House.

Police received multiple reports on the fire at around 5 a.m. A couple and their son were sent to the hospital for treatment. About 250 people were evacuated.
