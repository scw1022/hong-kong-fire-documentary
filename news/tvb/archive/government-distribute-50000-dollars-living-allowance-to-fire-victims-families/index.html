Government distribute 50,000 dollars living allowance to fire victims families

發佈日期: 2025-12-01 12:37
TVB News
已複製連結
Starting today, the government is distributing a living allowance of HK$50,000 per household to families affected by the fire at Wang Fuk Court. 

In recent days, multiple organizations have donated to support the victims. Among them, COSCO Shipping (Hong Kong) has donated HK$10 million, earmarked for emergency resettlement, medical assistance, and the supply of daily necessities for affected residents.
